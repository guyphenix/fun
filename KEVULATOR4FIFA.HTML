The Idea

You have a budget of 400 to use to pick your best 8 team line-up of the nations you think will excel at World Cup 2026.

Each team has been assigned a value based on their odds of doing well in the tournament. These range from heavy favourites like Spain (40) and France (38) down to massive underdogs like Haiti and Jordan (−16).

Your budget of 400 is spent by multiplying the team's value by the slot you put them in. For example, if you put Spain in your top ×8 slot, it uses up 320 of your budget (8 × 40). If you put an underdog like Haiti in your ×7 slot, it can help to balance your budget because you'll get value back into your budget (7 × −16 = −112). Balance your picks so you don't go bust on your budget!.

The deadline is 7pm 11th June 2026.

The Position Multiplier

Once you've submitted your selection of 8 teams, within budget, and the tournament begins, you get points each time your team plays in the real tournament. The points system for each match is explained further down this page, and then each game score for a team is multiplied by the slot you placed them in.

Example — the upside

Your top pick (x8) wins a group game 2–0.

Your score: Win (+3) + 2 goals scored (+4) + clean sheet (+1) = 8 points

Your multiplier score: 8 × 8 = +64 points

Example — the flip side

Your 7x pick lose a group game 0–3.

Your score: Loss (−3) + 0 goals scored (0) + 3 conceded (−3) = −6 points

Your multiplier score: −6 × 7 = −42 points

Tiebreaker

In the event of a tie on points, the tiebreaker is your prediction for the total number of yellow cards across all games in the tournament. Closest prediction wins.
